Connect your CRM to PG:AI to automatically sync your accounts, keep data up to date, and push enriched intelligence back into your existing workflow.

## Supported CRMs

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Salesforce" icon="cloud">
    Full bi-directional sync
  </Card>
  <Card title="HubSpot" icon="hubspot">
    Account and contact sync
  </Card>
  <Card title="Other CRMs" icon="plug">
    Via API or CSV import
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to integrations">
    Go to **Configuration > CRM Integration**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your CRM">
    Choose your CRM provider and authenticate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure sync settings">
    Select which objects to sync (accounts, contacts, opportunities) and set sync frequency.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Map fields">
    Map PG:AI fields to your CRM fields for consistent data flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test and activate">
    Run a test sync, verify the data, then activate the integration.
  </Step>
</Steps>
