## Prerequisites

<Note>
  Before using the AI Assistant, ensure you have:
  - A PG:AI account with **Agent access enabled**
  - At least one **company added** to your workspace with Intelligence data
</Note>

<Info>
  The AI Assistant uses Intelligence data as its foundation. The more enriched an account is, the better the assistant's responses will be.
</Info>

## Accessing the AI Assistant

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to a company">
    Navigate to any company in your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Agent tab">
    Click the **Agent** tab in the company view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start chatting">
    A conversation interface appears. You're ready to start  -  no additional setup is required. The assistant has automatic access to all Intelligence data for the account you're viewing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Starting a Conversation

Type a question or request in the message input. The assistant will respond using:

- **Intelligence data**  -  the account's strategic priorities, contacts, tech stack, financials, hiring data, and enrichment history.
- **Web search**  -  real-time web search for current information not yet in the Intelligence profile.
- **Deep research**  -  extended research triggered by specific requests (e.g. "research their recent acquisitions in detail").

<Tip>
  Responses include **source citations** linking back to the data or web pages used, so you can verify and explore further.
</Tip>

## Conversation Management

- **Multiple conversations**  -  you can create separate conversation threads per account for different research topics.
- **Conversation history**  -  all conversations persist and are accessible to your team. Previous research is not lost.
- **Conversation memory**  -  the assistant remembers context within a conversation. Follow-up questions build on previous answers.

## Configuration Options

The AI Assistant works out of the box with no configuration. Optional settings include:

- **Model selection**  -  choose between available LLM models for different quality/speed tradeoffs (if enabled for your organisation).
- **Memory settings**  -  control how much conversation history the assistant retains for context.
