## Prerequisites

<Note>
  Before setting up AgentMail, ensure you have:
  - A PG:AI account with **Agent access enabled**
  - An **email address or inbox** you want to connect
</Note>

## What AgentMail Does

AgentMail connects your email to the AI agent. Inbound emails are parsed, threaded, and made available as context. When you ask the agent to draft a follow-up or reference a previous conversation, it can draw from email context alongside Intelligence data.

## Setup

<Warning>
  AgentMail setup is managed at the organisation level by an admin.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to AgentMail settings">
    Navigate to **Settings → Integrations → AgentMail**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the inbound webhook">
    Configure the **inbound webhook** to receive emails.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Map email addresses">
    Map email addresses to PG:AI user accounts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test the connection">
    Test the connection by sending a test email. Once connected, inbound emails are automatically threaded and parsed. Attachments are stored and accessible to the agent.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Verification

After setup, verify the connection:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Send a test email">
    Send a test email to the configured address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Agent tab">
    Open the Agent tab on any account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify email access">
    Ask the agent: "Show me recent email threads." The test email should appear in the response.
  </Step>
</Steps>
