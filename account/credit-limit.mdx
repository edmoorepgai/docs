Credit Limits allow administrators to set individual credit allowances for each team member, providing granular control over resource usage and ensuring fair distribution of credits across your organization. This feature enables:

- Individual credit allocation for each user
- Fair distribution of resources across the team
- Better cost management and budget control
- Prevention of excessive credit usage by individual users

<Info>
  Credit Limits are configured by administrators in Settings → People → Credit Limits and apply to all credit-consuming activities within PG:AI.
</Info>

## Where You'll Find Credit Limits

- **Settings → People → Credit Limits**: Main configuration interface
- **User management**: Individual credit allocation settings
- **Usage monitoring**: Track individual credit consumption
- **Administrative controls**: Organization-wide credit management

## Setting Up Credit Limits

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Credit Limits">
    Navigate to Settings → People → Credit Limits in your admin dashboard
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Credit Limits">
    Turn on credit limits for your organization
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Individual Limits">
    Assign specific credit amounts to each user based on their needs
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor Usage">
    Track credit consumption and adjust limits as needed
  </Step>
</Steps>
