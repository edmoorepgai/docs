## Key Benefits

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="One place for everything" icon="layer-group">
    Account profile, contacts, financials, tech stack, hiring signals, and strategic insights in a single view. No more ten-tab research sessions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Always current" icon="clock">
    Continuous enrichment means data refreshes automatically. When a company posts new jobs, publishes earnings, or shifts strategy, the profile updates.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Deeper than firmographics" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Go beyond employee count and revenue. Understand what a company is investing in, who its competitors are, what its divisions focus on, and which roles it's hiring for.
  </Card>
  <Card title="From research to action" icon="bolt">
    The Sales Engagement module turns raw intelligence into meeting preparation: discovery questions, value pyramids, and tailored insight reports.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Shared knowledge" icon="users">
    Every rep sees the same intelligence. Research done by one person benefits the entire team. No more duplicated effort or lost context.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Feeds the platform" icon="link">
    Intelligence data flows into Territory scoring, Agent conversations, and Monitor alerts. It's the data foundation for everything else in PG:AI.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Who It's For

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Primary users">
    Account executives, strategic account managers, and business development reps - the people who research accounts, prepare for meetings, and need to understand a company before they pick up the phone.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Secondary users">
    Sales leaders who need visibility into account quality and rep preparedness. RevOps teams who want richer data flowing into territories and scoring models. Marketing teams who need account-level context for ABM campaigns and personalised outreach.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
