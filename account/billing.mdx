Billing settings let you purchase credits, monitor usage, and manage your PG:AI subscription. Track credit consumption to ensure uninterrupted access to the platform.

## How Credits Work

PG:AI uses a credit-based system:

<Info>
  **1 Credit = 1 Company Added**
</Info>

- **Credit consumption** - One credit per company added to PG:AI
- **Data validity** - Company data accessible for 12 months from addition
- **Team sharing** - All team members can access any added company
- **No replacements** - Credits cannot be reused once spent

## Credit Best Practices

<Warning>
  Since companies cannot be replaced once added, ensure accounts are confirmed prospects or strategic targets before using credits.
</Warning>

**Planning Tips:**

- Estimate monthly account research volume
- Buy in bulk for volume discounts
- Monitor usage to avoid running out unexpectedly
- Factor in seasonal peaks when purchasing
