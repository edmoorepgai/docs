Usage and Analytics provides comprehensive insights into how your team is using PG:AI, which accounts are receiving the most attention, and overall platform adoption metrics. Access detailed analytics to understand engagement patterns and measure the value PG:AI delivers to your sales process.

## What is Usage and Analytics?

Usage and Analytics consists of two main components that help administrators understand platform adoption and value:

- **Account Analytics**: Track engagement metrics for all accounts added by your teams
- **User Analytics**: Monitor individual and team-wide platform usage patterns
- **Activity Tracking**: View detailed logs of all actions taken within the platform

<Info>
  Usage and Analytics helps you measure PG:AI adoption, identify power users, and correlate platform usage with sales pipeline success.
</Info>

## Account Analytics

### **Understanding Account Engagement**

Account Analytics provides a comprehensive view of all accounts your teams have added to PG:AI, with detailed metrics to understand engagement levels:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Engagement Metrics" icon="chart-line">
    Total events, page views, active days, and unique user views per account
  </Card>
  <Card title="Timeline Data" icon="calendar">
    Date added, last activity date, months since added, days since last activity
  </Card>
  <Card title="Account Details" icon="building">
    Company industry, revenue category, employee count, and who added the account
  </Card>
  <Card title="Export Capabilities" icon="download">
    Filter by date range and export all data to CSV for further analysis
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### **Key Metrics Explained**

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Total Events">
    Count of all interactions with an account, including views, exports, and AI engagements
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Unique Users">
    Number of different team members who have accessed the account - higher numbers indicate collaborative selling
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Active Days">
    Total days where at least one team member engaged with the account
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Days Since Last Activity">
    Helps identify stale accounts that may need attention or removal
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## User Analytics

### **Analytics Overview Tab**

The overview provides high-level usage metrics:

- **Credit Usage**: Credits bought vs. credits used
- **Monthly Trends**: Track month-over-month changes in:
  - Companies added
  - Custom insights created
  - Content groups generated

### **User Analytics Table**

<Warning>
  Login counts may be lower than days active because users accessing PG:AI via shared email links don't technically "log in" during those sessions.
</Warning>

Track individual user engagement with detailed metrics:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Activity Metrics">
    - **Login Count**: Direct platform logins
    - **Days Active**: Total days with any platform activity
    - **Companies Added**: Number of accounts each user has added
    - **AI Uses**: Interactions with AI-powered features
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Engagement Data">
    - **Total Events**: All platform interactions
    - **Total Page Views**: Content consumption patterns
    - **Exports**: Number of data exports performed
    - **Date Tracking**: First seen and last seen dates
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### **Activity Tracker**

The Activity Tracker provides a detailed audit log of all platform actions:

- Company additions
- Custom insight creation
- Content exports
- AI feature usage
- Configuration changes

Filter activities by date range to investigate specific periods or user behaviors.

## Using Analytics for Sales Intelligence

### **Account Attribution Analysis**

Use Account Analytics to:

1. Identify which accounts receive the most team collaboration
2. Correlate high-engagement accounts with pipeline success
3. Find neglected accounts that need attention
4. Measure time-to-engagement for new accounts

### **User Adoption Tracking**

Monitor platform adoption by:

1. Identifying power users who can champion best practices
2. Finding users who need additional training
3. Tracking feature usage to understand what delivers value
4. Measuring team-wide engagement trends

## Data Export and Integration

### **CSV Export Capabilities**

Both Account and User Analytics support CSV exports for:

- Custom date range filtering
- Integration with BI tools
- Pipeline attribution analysis
- ROI calculations
- Team performance reviews

<Steps>
