The Tech Stack tab shows what technologies your target accounts actually use, based on analysis of job postings, online presence, and digital footprints. This intelligence helps you qualify technical fit, identify integration opportunities, and create credible technical conversations.

<Info>
Tech Stack data updates continuously as new job postings and technology mentions are discovered. All insights link back to their original sources for verification.
</Info>

## How to Use Tech Stack Data

**For Qualification:**
- Check if they use technologies you integrate with
- Identify gaps where your solution adds value
- Assess their technical sophistication level

**For Conversations:**
- "I noticed you're using Kubernetes extensively. How are you handling monitoring across your containerized apps?"
- "Your investment in Snowflake shows you're serious about analytics. What's driving your data strategy?"

**For Understanding Signal Strength:**
- Multiple recent job postings = active investment
- Senior roles mentioning tech = strategic importance
- Single mentions = possible experimentation
