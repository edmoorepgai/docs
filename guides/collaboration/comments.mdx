Comments and tags enable collaboration on account intelligence across your team.

## Adding Comments

1. Click on any insight to open the detailed sidebar
2. Navigate to the **Comments** section
3. Write your comment in the text field

## Tagging Colleagues

1. Use the **@** symbol to tag team members
2. Select the person you want to tag
3. Submit your comment

Tagged users receive notifications via email and in-app alerts.

## Use Cases

- **SDR to AE**: Highlight key insights for account strategy
- **AE to SDR**: Point out specific outreach opportunities
- **AE to CSM**: Share renewal-relevant intelligence
- **Team Collaboration**: Coordinate on account planning
