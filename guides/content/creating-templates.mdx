Content templates allow you to create reusable messaging that can be applied across accounts.

## Creating a Template

1. Navigate to the **Content** tab
2. Click **New Template** to open the template editor

## Template Configuration

Configure these settings for your template:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Content Settings">
    - **Type**: Email, notes, document, or call script
    - **Word Count**: Define the desired length
    - **Language**: Select output language
    - **Dataset**: Choose your data source
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Context Data">
    Select which insights to include:

    - Goals and strategy
    - Business priorities
    - Value pyramid insights
    - Custom insights
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Template Creation

1. **Title**: Give your template a descriptive name
2. **Instructions**: Write clear instructions for what you want PG:AI to generate
3. **Test Content**: Select a company to test the template output
4. **Save**: Template becomes available to your entire workspace
