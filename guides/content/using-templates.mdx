---
title: "Using Content Templates"
description: "Generate personalized content using your saved templates"
---

Once created, templates can be used in two locations within an account.

## From the Content Tab

1. Navigate to the **Content** tab within an account
2. Click **Draft Content**
3. Select your desired template
4. Content generates automatically for that company
5. **Save** the content or **Copy** to use externally

## From Individual Contacts

1. Go to the **Contacts** page within an account
2. Click into a specific contact
3. Select **Content** > **Draft Content**
4. Choose your template
5. Content generates specifically for that contact

<Tip>
  You can modify the context data for any generation by clicking **Context Data** and selecting different insights than the template default.
</Tip>
