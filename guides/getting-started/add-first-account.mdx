title: "Adding a Company"
description: "Learn how to add new companies to PG:AI for account intelligence generation"
---

When you first log into PG:AI, you'll land on the Accounts page. There are two ways to add a company:

## Method 1: Search Bar

1. Type a company name in the search bar at the top of the page
2. Select from matching companies in our database
3. Click the **plus icon** in the purple box to add the account

## Method 2: Add Company Button

1. Click the **Add Company** button in the top right
2. Search for the company in the modal
3. Click **plus** to add it to your accounts
