Engage Settings control how PG:AI generates Value Pyramids, Three Whys frameworks, and Discovery Questions on the Engage tab. These settings ensure your value proposition is consistently and accurately mapped to each account's specific strategic priorities and business challenges.

## What are Engage Settings?

Engage Settings are configurations that train PG:AI to understand your solution and create targeted engagement content:

- Define your solution's core capabilities and value propositions
- Specify business challenges and strategic initiatives you address
- Set search signals that identify relevant account information
- Connect your datasets to power personalized content generation

<Info>
  Engage Settings directly determine the quality and relevance of your Value Pyramids, Three Whys, and Discovery Questions. Well-configured settings produce more compelling and accurate sales content.
</Info>

## Why Engage Settings Matter

### **Content Relevance and Quality**

Proper configuration ensures:

- Value Pyramids accurately connect account strategies to your solution capabilities
- Three Whys frameworks reflect your actual competitive advantages and differentiation
- Discovery Questions uncover pain points your solution specifically addresses
- Generated content uses your proven messaging and positioning

### **Sales Team Consistency**

Well-defined settings provide:

- Uniform value proposition delivery across all team members
- Consistent competitive positioning and differentiation messaging
- Standardized approach to connecting solution benefits to customer needs
- Scalable framework that works regardless of individual rep experience

## Configuration Process

### **Setting Up Your Engage Configuration**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Name Your Configuration">
    Create descriptive names like "Enterprise Financial Services" or "Mid-Market Manufacturing"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write Solution Description">
    Provide comprehensive overview of your solution's capabilities, challenges addressed, and strategic value
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define Search Signals">
    Add keywords that identify relevant account initiatives, challenges, and strategic priorities
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Datasets">
    Choose which uploaded sales collateral to reference for content generation
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test and Refine">
    Generate sample content and adjust settings based on quality and relevance
  </Step>
</Steps>

### **Solution Description Best Practices**

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Structure and Format">
    **Recommended Structure:**

    - **Core Capabilities**: What your solution does and how it works
    - **Challenges Addressed**: Specific business problems you solve
    - **Strategic Initiatives**: Transformation goals and outcomes you enable
    - **Implementation Benefits**: Operational improvements and measurable value

    Organize with clear sections for "Challenges" and "Strategies/Initiatives" to help PG:AI understand your value proposition.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Content Guidelines">
    **Include:**

    - Specific business outcomes and measurable benefits
    - Technical capabilities that differentiate your solution
    - Industry-specific value propositions and use cases
    - Integration capabilities and implementation advantages

    **Avoid:**

    - Mentioning your company name (focus on solution value)
    - Generic marketing language without specific benefits
    - Technical jargon without business context
    - Competitor comparisons or negative positioning
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### **Search Signals Strategy**

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Business Initiative Signals">
    Keywords that identify strategic transformation efforts:

    - Digital transformation, operational efficiency, cost optimization
    - Customer experience improvement, revenue growth initiatives
    - Risk management, compliance programs, security enhancements
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Technology Area Signals">
    Terms related to your solution's technical domain:

    - AI & ML initiatives, cloud migration, data modernization
    - Infrastructure modernization, DevOps transformation
    - Automation, integration, platform consolidation
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Challenge and Pain Point Signals">
    Problems and obstacles your solution addresses:

    - Manual processes, operational inefficiencies, system limitations
    - Security vulnerabilities, compliance gaps, integration challenges
    - Scalability constraints, performance bottlenecks, cost pressures
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Industry-Specific Signals">
    Terminology and concepts relevant to your target markets:

    - Regulatory compliance, industry standards, sector-specific challenges
    - Market trends, competitive pressures, customer expectations
    - Technology adoption patterns, implementation requirements
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Multiple Configuration Groups

### **When to Create Different Settings**

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Industry Verticals">
    Create separate configurations for different industries:

    - Financial Services: Regulatory compliance, risk management, customer experience
    - Healthcare: Patient outcomes, compliance, interoperability, cost reduction
    - Manufacturing: Operational efficiency, supply chain, quality management
    - Retail: Customer experience, inventory optimization, omnichannel capabilities
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Company Segments">
    Tailor settings for different company sizes and types:

    - Enterprise: Complex implementations, integration requirements, scalability
    - Mid-Market: Cost efficiency, rapid deployment, operational simplification
    - SMB: Ease of use, quick wins, budget optimization
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Use Cases">
    Different configurations for various solution applications:

    - New Logo: Competitive differentiation, implementation benefits, risk mitigation
    - Expansion: Additional capabilities, integration opportunities, ROI optimization
    - Replacement: Migration benefits, improvement metrics, competitive advantages
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### **Configuration Management**

<Warning>
  Regularly review and update your Engage Settings based on successful sales conversations and market feedback. Outdated configurations will generate less relevant content.
</Warning>

**Best Practices:**

- **Regular Updates**: Refresh signals and descriptions quarterly
- **Success Analysis**: Analyze which settings generate the most effective content
- **Team Feedback**: Incorporate sales team input on content quality and relevance
- **Market Evolution**: Adjust for changing customer priorities and competitive landscape
