## What are Personas?

Personas help you identify and engage with the right people at your target accounts by defining groups of people who share common job functions, responsibilities, or characteristics.

Personas are defined groups of people within organizations that share common job functions, responsibilities, or characteristics. Examples include:

- DevOps teams
- Sales leaders
- Security professionals
- HR managers
- Marketing executives

## How Persona Settings Work

### 1. Adding a Persona

To create a new persona, click "Add Persona" and provide:

- **Name of the group** - A descriptive name for this persona
- **Department** - The department they belong to
- **Maximum contacts** - The number of contacts you want PG:AI to find

### 2. Configuration Components

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Job Levels">
    Choose applicable job levels:

    - Manager
    - VP
    - Director
    - Executive
    - Individual Contributor
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define Relevant Keywords">
    Add job titles or functions like:

    - DevSecOps
    - Cyber Security
    - Software Engineering
    - Data Analytics
    - Product Management
  </Step>
  <Step title="Understanding Combinations">
    PG:AI will search for every combination of levels and keywords. For example:

    - HR Manager
    - HR Director
    - HR VP
    - Security Manager
    - Security Director
  </Step>
</Steps>

### 3. Dataset Selection

Choose which dataset to use when building content for this persona. This ensures the generated content is relevant to their specific role and responsibilities.

### 4. Additional Keywords

Define terms to help identify specific insights relevant to the persona. These keywords help PG:AI surface the most pertinent information for each role.

### 5. Default Settings

<Note>
  The platform automatically sets up three default personas to help you get started. You can modify these or create your own based on your ideal customer profile.
</Note>

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  Create targeted personas that align with your ideal customer profile to ensure PG:AI finds the most relevant contacts at your target accounts.
</Tip>

### Recommended Approach:

1. Start with your most common buyer personas
2. Be specific with job titles and keywords
3. Set appropriate contact limits based on company size
4. Regularly review and refine based on results
5. Create separate personas for different stages of the buying process
