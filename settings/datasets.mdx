## What are Datasets?

Datasets are collections of information about your company, products, and value propositions that PG:AI uses to generate personalized content and insights for your target accounts.

Datasets are structured collections of information that serve as the foundation for PG:AI's content generation. They contain details about your:

- Company overview and history
- Products and services
- Value propositions
- Competitive differentiators
- Customer success stories
- Industry expertise

## Types of Datasets

### 1. Core Company Dataset

Your foundational dataset that includes:

- Company mission and vision
- Core products and services
- Key differentiators
- Target markets
- Company achievements

### 2. Product/Solution Datasets

Detailed information about specific offerings:

- Feature descriptions
- Benefits and outcomes
- Technical specifications
- Pricing models
- Implementation details

### 3. Industry-Specific Datasets

Tailored content for vertical markets:

- Industry-specific value propositions
- Relevant case studies
- Compliance and regulatory expertise
- Industry terminology and challenges

### 4. Persona-Based Datasets

Content optimized for different roles:

- Executive-level business outcomes
- Technical implementation details
- End-user benefits
- Economic buyer ROI metrics

## Managing Datasets

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Dataset Settings">
    Navigate to Settings \> Organisation Settings \> Datasets
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New Dataset">
    Click "Add Dataset" and provide:

    - Dataset name
    - Description
    - Category/type
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Content">
    Input your content using:

    - Text editor for direct input
    - File upload for documents
    - Integration with existing content repositories
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Usage">
    Define where and how this dataset should be used:

    - Assign to specific workflows
    - Link to personas
    - Set for specific industries
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

### Content Guidelines

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Do's">
    - Use clear, concise language
    - Include specific metrics and outcomes
    - Provide real customer examples
    - Update regularly with new wins
    - Organize content logically
    - Use consistent formatting
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Don'ts">
    - Avoid jargon without explanation
    - Don't include outdated information
    - Avoid competitor bashing
    - Don't use internal acronyms
    - Avoid overly technical language for business datasets
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Dataset Organization

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Naming Conventions">
    Use clear, descriptive names:

    - `Core_Company_Overview_2024`
    - `Product_CloudSolution_Enterprise`
    - `Industry_FinancialServices_Compliance`
    - `Persona_CISO_Security`
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Version Control">
    - Maintain version history
    - Date significant updates
    - Archive outdated content
    - Document major changes
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Content Structure">
    Organize information hierarchically:

    1. Executive summary
    2. Detailed descriptions
    3. Proof points
    4. Supporting materials
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Tips for Success

<Info>
  **Regular Updates**: Schedule quarterly reviews of your datasets to ensure information remains current and relevant.
</Info>

### Quality Checklist

- Information is current (within last 6 months)
- Metrics and statistics are accurate
- Customer examples have approval for use
- Content aligns with current messaging
- Technical details are verified
- Compliance statements are up-to-date

### Integration with Other Features

Datasets work seamlessly with:

- **Workflows**: Automatically apply relevant datasets
- **Personas**: Match content to specific roles
- **Custom Insights**: Provide context for analysis
- **Engage Settings**: Power value proposition mapping

<Warning>
  **Data Governance**: Ensure all dataset content complies with your company's data governance policies and customer confidentiality agreements.
</Warning>
