PG:AI V2 is a ground-up rebuild of the platform. New backend, new frontend, new capabilities. This page covers everything that's changed and what it means for your workflow.

## The big picture

We rebuilt PG:AI from scratch on a new backend architecture designed to be composable, scalable, and agent-native. This isn't a UI refresh - it's a fundamentally different platform that enables everything below, plus a roadmap we couldn't have shipped on V1.

The result: faster enrichment, a dramatically better AI agent, a composable content layer, territory-level planning, and a unified workspace that pulls everything together.

## Studio

Studio is where intelligence becomes action. Every other module in PG:AI builds intelligence - Territory scores your accounts, Account gives you deep research, Contact maps the people, Monitoring watches for changes. Studio is where you use all of that to actually do something.

### AI Agent

{/* GIF PLACEHOLDER: Agent answering a research question with citations */}

A brand-new, dramatically more capable AI agent. This isn't the Q&A assistant from V1 - it's a research agent that can work autonomously on your behalf: running multi-step research, pulling from live data sources, generating content, and taking actions across your accounts.

<Info>
The agent has full access to every intelligence module for the account you're viewing - strategic priorities, contacts, tech stack, financials, hiring data, enrichment history, and previous conversations. Every response is grounded in what PG:AI actually knows.
</Info>

### Canvas

{/* IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Canvas with structured content */}

A composable content surface where you generate, edit, and refine deliverables. Canvases can contain free-form text or structured UI components - and you can edit them yourself or collaborate with the agent, similar to working with an AI-powered IDE.

Use cases include account briefs, engagement plans, email sequences, meeting prep docs, discovery question sets, competitive comparisons, and anything else a GTM team member would need to produce.

### Canvas templates

Pre-built and custom templates for common GTM workflows:

- Build a value pyramid for an account
- Match your use cases to a company's stated strategies
- Generate email sequences grounded in account intelligence
- Create meeting prep documents
- Produce competitive battle cards

You can use the built-in templates or create your own that your team can reuse.

### Workflows

{/* IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Workflow builder or execution view */}

Multi-step, configurable AI pipelines that run research, analysis, and content generation across accounts at scale. Instead of doing things one account at a time, workflows let you define a sequence of steps - research, analyse, generate - and run it across your entire territory.

## Monitoring

{/* IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Monitoring feed with ranked signals */}

PG:AI Monitoring watches your accounts for changes and alerts you when something happens. Configurable monitoring agents track hiring shifts, strategic changes, competitive moves, financial events, and more - so your team never misses a signal.

Enterprise sellers manage portfolios of 50–200+ accounts. It's impossible to manually track what's happening at every company, every day. Monitor replaces ad-hoc checking - Google Alerts, LinkedIn notifications, manual scans - with systematic, contextual monitoring powered by your Intelligence data.

Signals are ranked by relevance, not just recency, so the things that actually matter surface first.
