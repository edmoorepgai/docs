## What Makes PG:AI Different

Unlike generic AI tools, PG:AI is purpose-built for B2B sales:

- **Comprehensive Analysis**: Processes 1,000-5,000 pages per account (vs. ~5 for ChatGPT)
- **Your Value Proposition**: Trained on your specific solutions, use cases, and messaging
- **Automatic Updates**: Continuously monitors accounts for new initiatives and changes
- **Sales-Ready Outputs**: Generates value pyramids, discovery questions, and personalized content
