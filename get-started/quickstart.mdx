# Quickstart Guide

Get your first account intelligence in minutes and see how PG:AI transforms your sales process.

<Info>
  **Prerequisites**: You'll need a PG:AI account. If you don't have one yet, [Get Access Here](https://www.getpg.ai/get-access).
</Info>

## Step 1: Access PG:AI

1. Go to [app.getpg.ai](https://www.app.getpg.ai/login)
2. Log in with your credentials
3. You'll land on the dashboard showing your account overview

<Frame>
  ![PG:AI Dashboard](/images/app-accounts.png)
</Frame>

## Step 2: Add Your First Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Add Company'">
    Find the "Add Company" button in the top right corner
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Company Name">
    Type the company name (e.g., "HSBC" or "Walmart")
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and Process">
    Click "Add" - PG:AI will begin analyzing thousands of data sources
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Each company uses 1 credit and takes 5-10 minutes to process. You'll receive a notification when ready.
</Note>

## Step 3: Explore Account Intelligence

Once processed, click on the company to see:

### **Company Insights**

- **Strategic Priorities**: High-level business objectives
- **Goals & Initiatives**: Specific programs and targets
- **SWOT Analysis**: Strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, threats
- **Organization Structure**: Divisions and key business units

### **Engage Insights**

- **Value Pyramid**: How your solution maps to their priorities
- **Three Whys**: Why change, why now, why you
- **Discovery Questions**: Targeted questions for meetings
- **Custom Insights**: Ask specific questions about the account

## Step 5: Try Advanced Features

### **AI Assistant**

Ask specific questions about the account:

- "What are their main challenges with digital transformation?"
- "How does our solution align with their 2024 initiatives?"
- "What recent investments have they made in technology?"

### **Content Generation**

1. Go to **Content** tab
2. Select a template (email, call script, etc.)
3. Customize with specific insights
4. Copy and use in your outreach

### **Technology Stack**

Review their current technologies to:

- Identify integration opportunities
- Spot replacement possibilities
- Understand their technical environment
