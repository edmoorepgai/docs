Organization Structure provides detailed insights into how companies organize their business units, divisions, and reporting relationships, including key executives and decision-makers within each organizational segment.

Organization Structure maps the operational framework of companies from official company communications:

- Annual reports and investor communications
- Earnings calls and executive presentations
- Strategic planning documents and board communications
- Public statements and press releases

<Info>
  Organization Structure is compiled from annual reports, organizational charts, executive profiles, and corporate communications that detail business unit operations and leadership.
</Info>

## Where You'll See Organization Structure

- **Overview tab**: Key divisions and executive leadership
- **Contacts tab**: Division-specific leadership and contacts
- **Custom Insights**: When searching for organizational information
- **AI Assistant**: Referenced in organizational navigation queries

Organization Structure is compiled from:

- **Annual reports and investor communications**
- **Earnings calls and executive presentations** 
- **Strategic planning documents and board communications**

The system analyzes organizational information to map business divisions, leadership structure, and reporting relationships.
