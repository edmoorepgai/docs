## Overview

Organisation Structure provides a comprehensive view of the company's divisions, business units, leadership team, and reporting relationships extracted from official sources.

## Why It Matters

Understanding organizational structure helps you:
- Navigate complex buying committees
- Identify the right stakeholders
- Understand decision-making processes
- Target specific divisions with relevant messaging
