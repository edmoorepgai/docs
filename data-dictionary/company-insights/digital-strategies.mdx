Digital Strategies are the technology-focused initiatives and transformation plans that companies implement to modernize operations, enhance capabilities, and drive competitive advantage through digital innovation.

Digital Strategies represent the technology-driven approaches companies use to transform their business:

- Annual reports and investor communications
- Earnings calls and executive presentations
- Strategic planning documents and board communications
- Public statements and press releases

<Info>
  Digital Strategies are identified from technology-related discussions in earnings calls, digital transformation announcements, IT investment communications, and strategic technology partnerships.
</Info>

## Where You'll See Digital Strategies

- **Overview tab**: Featured in the main account summary
- **Custom Insights**: When searching for technology initiatives
- **Engage tab**: Used in technology alignment positioning
- **AI Assistant**: Referenced in digital transformation queries

## How Digital Strategies Are Generated

Digital Strategies are identified through AI analysis of:

- **Annual reports and investor communications**
- **Earnings calls and executive presentations** 
- **Strategic planning documents and board communications**
- **Public statements and press releases**

The system identifies technology-focused initiatives and transformation plans that companies consistently emphasize.
