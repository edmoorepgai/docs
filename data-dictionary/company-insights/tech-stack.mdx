Tech Stack provides comprehensive insights into your target accounts' technology infrastructure by analyzing job postings, online data, and other digital footprints to reveal what technologies they actively use and invest in.

Tech Stack intelligence is built from multiple data sources that reveal actual technology usage:

- Job postings and technical requirements
- Online presence and technical documentation
- Digital footprint and web applications
- Technology-related announcements and initiatives

<Info>
  Tech Stack data is continuously updated as new information becomes available, ensuring you have the most current view of their technology landscape.
</Info>

## Where You'll See Tech Stack

- **Tech Stack tab**: Complete technology landscape view
- **Custom Insights**: When searching for specific technologies
- **Engage tab**: Referenced in technical alignment discussions
- **AI Assistant**: Used for technology-related queries

## How Tech Stack Is Generated

Tech Stack intelligence is compiled from:

- **Job postings and technical requirements**
- **Online presence and technical documentation**
- **Digital footprint and web applications**
- **Technology-related announcements and initiatives**

The system analyzes technology mentions to build comprehensive technology profiles.
