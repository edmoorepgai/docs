## Confidence Indicators

<Steps>
  <Step title="High Confidence (90%+)">
    - Multiple recent job postings
    - Senior roles requiring expertise
    - Certification requirements
  </Step>
  <Step title="Medium Confidence (60-89%)">
    - Some job mentions
    - Related technology indicators
    - Partner announcements
  </Step>
  <Step title="Low Confidence (<60%)">
    - Few or old references
    - Indirect mentions only
    - Conflicting signals
  </Step>
</Steps>
