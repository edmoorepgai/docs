## Overview

SWOT Analysis provides a structured assessment of the company's strategic position based on comprehensive analysis of their public documents and statements.

## Framework

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Strengths">
    - Core competencies
    - Market advantages
    - Financial position
    - Unique assets
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Weaknesses">
    - Operational challenges
    - Technology gaps
    - Resource constraints
    - Market limitations
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Opportunities">
    - Growth markets
    - Digital initiatives
    - Strategic partnerships
    - Innovation areas
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Threats">
    - Competitive pressures
    - Market disruptions
    - Regulatory risks
    - Technology shifts
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Sales Strategy

Leverage SWOT insights to:
- **Strengths**: Align with their advantages
- **Weaknesses**: Position your solution as addressing gaps
- **Opportunities**: Show how you enable growth
- **Threats**: Demonstrate risk mitigation
