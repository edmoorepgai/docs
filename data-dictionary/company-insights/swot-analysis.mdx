## The SWOT Framework

<Grid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Strengths" icon="shield" color="#10b981">
    **What they do well**

    * Core competencies
    * Market advantages
    * Unique assets
    * Financial position
  </Card>

  <Card title="Weaknesses" icon="shield-exclamation" color="#ef4444">
    **Where they struggle**

    * Operational gaps
    * Tech debt
    * Resource constraints
    * Process inefficiencies
  </Card>

  <Card title="Opportunities" icon="rocket" color="#3b82f6">
    **Where they can grow**

    * Market expansion
    * Digital initiatives
    * New products
    * Partnerships
  </Card>

  <Card title="Threats" icon="warning" color="#f59e0b">
    **What keeps them up**

    * Competition
    * Disruption
    * Regulation
    * Market shifts
  </Card>
</Grid>

## Sales Strategy Matrix

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Align to Strengths">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Identify Core Strengths">
        Find what they're proud of and protect
      </Step>
      <Step title="Position as Enhancer">
        "Make your strength even stronger"
      </Step>
      <Step title="Case Study">
        ```
        Strength: "Market-leading customer service"
        Position: "Our solution helps you maintain that lead by enabling 50% faster response times"
        ```
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Address Weaknesses">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Acknowledge Carefully">
        Never call out weaknesses directly
      </Step>
      <Step title="Position as Solution">
        "Transform challenges into advantages"
      </Step>
      <Step title="Case Study">
        ```
        Weakness: "Legacy technology infrastructure"
        Position: "Our platform modernizes incrementally without disrupting operations"
        ```
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Enable Opportunities">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Connect to Initiatives">
        Link opportunities to funded projects
      </Step>
      <Step title="Position as Accelerator">
        "Capture opportunities faster"
      </Step>
      <Step title="Case Study">
        ```
        Opportunity: "Expansion into APAC markets"
        Position: "Our solution scales globally and already supports 15 APAC languages"
        ```
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mitigate Threats">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Create Urgency">
        Threats drive immediate action
      </Step>
      <Step title="Position as Shield">
        "Protect against disruption"
      </Step>
      <Step title="Case Study">
        ```
        Threat: "New digital-first competitors"
        Position: "Our platform helps you match their agility while leveraging your scale"
        ```
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
