## Understanding Peer Dynamics

Peer Analysis reveals how your prospect views their competitive landscape, enabling you to:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Position Against Competitors">
    Use their language about differentiation
  </Step>
  <Step title="Identify Industry Pressure">
    Understand what keeps them up at night
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Displacement Opportunities">
    See where competitors are vulnerable
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Competitive Urgency">
    Show how peers are pulling ahead
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Intelligence Categories

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="🏆 Positioning">
    **How they see themselves:**

    - Market leadership claims
    - Unique differentiators
    - Competitive advantages
    - Strategic moats

    <Card title="Example Usage" icon="lightbulb">
      "You mentioned being the 'innovation leader' vs [Competitor].
      Our platform helps maintain that edge by..."
    </Card>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="⚔️ Threats">
    **Who they worry about:**

    - Direct competitors
    - New entrants
    - Substitute solutions
    - Market disruptors

    <Warning>
      Use competitive intelligence carefully - focus on your strengths, not competitor weaknesses
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="📊 Benchmarks">
    **How they measure up:**

    - Market share data
    - Growth comparisons
    - Capability gaps
    - Performance metrics

    <Info>
      Benchmarking data creates natural ROI discussions
    </Info>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
