## Overview

Peer Analysis examines how the target company positions itself against competitors and industry peers based on their public statements and strategic communications.

## Analysis Includes

- **Competitive Positioning**: How they differentiate from peers
- **Market Share**: Their position in key markets
- **Strategic Advantages**: Claimed competitive advantages
- **Industry Challenges**: Common challenges faced by peers

## Application

Use peer analysis to:
1. Understand competitive dynamics
2. Position against incumbent solutions
3. Identify industry-wide pain points
4. Leverage competitive displacement opportunities

<Warning>
Peer analysis is based on public statements and may not reflect complete competitive dynamics.
</Warning>
