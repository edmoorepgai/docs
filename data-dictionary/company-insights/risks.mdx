## Risk Mitigation Positioning

<MitigationFramework>
  <Framework>
    <FrameworkTitle>The Insurance Angle</FrameworkTitle>

    <FrameworkSteps>
      <Step>Quantify the risk exposure ($X impact)</Step>
      <Step>Show probability of occurrence (Y% likely)</Step>
      <Step>Calculate expected value of risk</Step>
      <Step>Position solution cost as insurance premium</Step>
    </FrameworkSteps>

    <Example>
      "The $10M compliance risk has a 70% probability based on peer failures.
      Our solution is 2% of that exposure - essentially insurance that pays for itself."
    </Example>
  </Framework>

  <Framework>
    <FrameworkTitle>The Opportunity Cost Play</FrameworkTitle>

    <FrameworkSteps>
      <Step>Identify what risk prevents them from doing</Step>
      <Step>Quantify missed opportunity value</Step>
      <Step>Show how removing risk enables growth</Step>
      <Step>Calculate total value (risk avoided + opportunity captured)</Step>
    </FrameworkSteps>

    <Example>
      "Your scalability constraints aren't just a risk - they're preventing
      you from capturing the 40% market growth. That's $50M in missed revenue."
    </Example>
  </Framework>
</MitigationFramework>

## Discovery Questions for Risks

<QuestionBank>
  <QuestionCategory title="Exploratory Questions">
    * "What keeps you up at night about the business?"
    * "What could derail your strategic plans?"
    * "Where are you most vulnerable to competition?"
    * "What would happen if \[specific risk] materialized?"
  </QuestionCategory>

  <QuestionCategory title="Quantification Questions">
    * "What would be the financial impact if \[risk] occurred?"
    * "How likely do you think \[risk] is to happen?"
    * "What's your exposure if you miss \[deadline]?"
    * "How much are you spending to manage \[risk] today?"
  </QuestionCategory>

  <QuestionCategory title="Urgency Questions">
    * "When does this become critical?"
    * "What triggers would make this urgent?"
    * "How long can you operate with this risk?"
    * "What's your contingency plan?"
  </QuestionCategory>
</QuestionBank>
