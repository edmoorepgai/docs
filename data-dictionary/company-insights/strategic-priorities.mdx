## How PG:AI Extracts Priorities

<Steps>
  <Step title="Document Analysis">
    Processes annual reports, 10-Ks, earnings transcripts & web pages
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initiative Detection">
    AI identifies strategic language patterns and commitments
  </Step>
  <Step title="Priority Ranking">
    Weights initiatives by executive mentions and investment
  </Step>
  <Step title="Timeline Mapping">
    Extracts deadlines and milestone dates
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sales Application

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Discovery Questions" icon="message-question">
    - "I noticed your CEO mentioned [priority] as critical for 2025. What's driving that timeline?"
    - "How is [priority] impacting your team's current projects?"
    - "What happens if you miss the Q3 deadline for [initiative]?"
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Executive Messaging" icon="user-tie">
    - Reference specific board commitments
    - Align your solution to stated outcomes
    - Create urgency using their timeline
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Competitive Positioning" icon="chess">
    - "Unlike generic solutions, we specifically accelerate [their priority]"
    - "Our track record with [similar priority] at [peer company]"
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
