## Overview

Contacts provides a curated list of relevant stakeholders within the target organization, including executives, technical leaders, and key decision-makers.

## Contact Types

- **Executive Leaders**: C-suite and VP-level contacts
- **Technical Leaders**: CTOs, architects, engineering leads
- **Functional Heads**: Department and division leaders
- **Key Influencers**: Notable team members in relevant areas
