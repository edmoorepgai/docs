## Overview

Goals represent the specific, measurable objectives that companies have publicly stated in their investor communications, annual reports, and strategic plans.

## What's Included

- **Financial Targets**: Revenue, growth, and profitability goals
- **Operational Objectives**: Efficiency and performance targets
- **Market Expansion**: Geographic and segment growth plans
- **Transformation Goals**: Digital and business model targets

## How to Use

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Goals">
    Examine the company's stated objectives
  </Step>
  <Step title="Map Solutions">
    Connect your offerings to their goal achievement
  </Step>
  <Step title="Quantify Impact">
    Show how you accelerate their progress
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

<Tip>
Focus on goals with specific timelines and metrics - these indicate urgency and budget allocation.
</Tip>
