## Understanding Company Goals

Goals are specific, measurable objectives that companies publicly commit to achieving. These create powerful sales conversations because they:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Have Budget" icon="dollar-sign">
    Stated goals come with allocated resources
  </Card>
  <Card title="Create Urgency" icon="clock">
    Public deadlines drive action
  </Card>
  <Card title="Define Success" icon="trophy">
    Clear metrics for ROI discussions
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to Leverage Goals

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify Relevant Goals">
    Find goals that your solution directly impacts
  </Step>
  <Step title="Quantify Your Impact">
    ```
    Their Goal: "Reduce operational costs by 20% by year-end"
    Your Impact: "Our solution delivers 25% cost reduction in 6 months"
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Urgency">
    Reference their timeline and what happens if they miss it
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build Business Case">
    Show ROI using their metrics, not yours
  </Step>
</Steps>
