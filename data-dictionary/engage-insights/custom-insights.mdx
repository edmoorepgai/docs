Custom Insights provide specialized intelligence tailored to your unique business focus areas, allowing you to track and analyze account activities, initiatives, and mentions related to your specific solution domains and value propositions.

Custom Insights are personalized intelligence categories based on your configured criteria:

- Annual reports and investor communications
- Earnings calls and executive presentations
- Strategic planning documents and board communications
- Public statements and press releases

## Where You'll See Custom Insights

- **Custom Insights tab**: View all configured custom intelligence
- **Overview tab**: Summary of relevant custom insights
- **AI Assistant**: Query specific custom insight findings
- **Content generation**: Use insights for targeted messaging

## How Custom Insights Are Generated

Custom Insights are created through analysis of:

- **Annual reports and investor communications**
- **Earnings calls and executive presentations**
- **Strategic planning documents and board communications**
- **Public statements and press releases**

The system searches for your configured criteria and themes across these sources.
