Custom Insights provide specialized intelligence tailored to your unique business focus areas, allowing you to track and analyze account activities, initiatives, and mentions related to your specific solution domains and value propositions.

## What are Custom Insights?

Custom Insights are personalized intelligence categories that you define based on your business needs:

- Solution-specific tracking (cloud security, DevOps, customer experience, etc.)
- Industry-specific intelligence relevant to your market focus
- Technology-specific mentions and initiatives
- Custom-defined business themes and strategic areas

## Why Custom Insights Matter for Sales

### **Solution-Specific Intelligence**

Custom Insights enable you to:

- Track mentions and initiatives directly related to your value proposition
- Identify accounts actively discussing problems your solution solves
- Monitor competitive landscape in your specific solution area
- Understand market timing and readiness for your type of solution

### **Competitive Advantage**

Custom tracking provides:

- Intelligence that competitors without custom setup cannot easily access
- Deep domain expertise demonstration in your solution areas
- Ability to engage at exactly the right moment when needs emerge
- Differentiated conversation starters based on specialized knowledge

## How to Use Custom Insights

### **Strategic Positioning**

**Solution Alignment:**
"I noticed you mentioned [Custom Insight Topic] in your recent announcement. Our solution specifically addresses..."

**Market Timing:**
"Given your focus on [Custom Insight Area], this seems like the perfect time to discuss how we can accelerate those initiatives..."

**Competitive Differentiation:**
"Unlike general solutions, we specialize specifically in [Custom Insight Domain], which aligns perfectly with your [Initiative]..."

### **Conversation Starters**

- **Initiative Discovery**: "I saw your team is working on [Custom Insight]. What's driving that priority?"
- **Challenge Exploration**: "How are you approaching [Custom Insight Challenge] given your industry constraints?"
- **Success Metrics**: "What does success look like for your [Custom Insight Initiative]?"
- **Timeline Understanding**: "What's your timeline for the [Custom Insight Project] you mentioned?"

## Best Practices

<Warning>
  Custom Insights are only as valuable as the criteria you define. Ensure your custom categories align closely with genuine business priorities and buying signals relevant to your solution.
</Warning>

1. **Strategic Relevance**: Define insights that directly connect to your value proposition
2. **Specificity Matters**: Create focused categories rather than broad, generic themes  
3. **Regular Review**: Update custom insight definitions as your solution and market evolve
4. **Source Validation**: Always check sources to understand context and accuracy
5. **Competitive Intelligence**: Use insights to understand competitive positioning in your space

## Setting Up Custom Insights

### **Configuration Requirements**

To maximize Custom Insight value:

1. **Define Clear Categories**: Create specific, focused insight areas
2. **Set Relevant Keywords**: Include terminology common in your solution space
3. **Specify Sources**: Identify which types of sources are most valuable
4. **Regular Updates**: Refine categories based on market changes and solution evolution
5. **Team Alignment**: Ensure categories align with overall GTM strategy

### **Best Practice Categories**

Consider creating custom insights for:

- **Solution-Specific Terms**: Technology, processes, or capabilities you provide
- **Problem Areas**: Challenges and pain points your solution addresses
- **Industry Terminology**: Sector-specific language and concepts
- **Competitive Landscape**: Mentions of competitors or alternative approaches
- **Buying Signals**: Language that indicates purchase readiness or budget allocation
