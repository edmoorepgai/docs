## Overview

Custom Insights allow you to request specific analysis or information about a company that goes beyond standard reports. Each account includes 5 custom insight credits.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Define Your Query">
    Specify exactly what information or analysis you need
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Request">
    Use one of your custom insight credits
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Analysis">
    PG:AI analyzes available data to answer your query
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive Insights">
    Get tailored intelligence specific to your request
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example Requests

- "Analyze their sustainability initiatives and ESG commitments"
- "Identify all mentions of API modernization projects"
- "Compare their digital strategy to their main competitor"
- "Find evidence of budget allocation for security initiatives"
