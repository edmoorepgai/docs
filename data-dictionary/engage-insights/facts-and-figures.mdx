Facts & Figures are specific, quantifiable data points extracted from your target accounts' public communications that support your value proposition, create conversation credibility, and provide concrete context for business discussions.

Facts & Figures are measurable, verifiable data points from official sources:

- Annual reports and investor communications
- Earnings calls and executive presentations
- Strategic planning documents and board communications
- Public statements and press releases

<Info>
  Facts & Figures are automatically extracted from annual reports, earnings calls, strategic communications, and other official sources to ensure accuracy and credibility.
</Info>

## Where You'll See Facts & Figures

- **Engage tab**: Supporting data for value propositions and business cases
- **Content generation**: Credibility-building data for outreach and presentations
- **AI Assistant**: Reference specific metrics in strategic conversations
- **Overview tab**: Key metrics and data points summary

The system identifies specific, measurable data points that provide business context and credibility.
