Content transforms account intelligence into actionable sales materials, enabling you to create personalized emails, call scripts, meeting notes, and other sales content based on your target account's specific priorities and challenges.

Content is generated using account intelligence combined with your configured templates and messaging frameworks:

- Strategic priorities and business objectives
- Goals, challenges, and digital strategies  
- Technology stack and organizational structure
- Custom insights and recent developments

<Info>
  All content is generated based on templates configured in Settings and personalized using the specific account intelligence gathered by PG:AI.
</Info>

## Where You'll See Content

- **Content tab**: Generate and manage all sales content
- **AI Assistant**: Request specific content types and recommendations
- **Engage tab**: Access content creation from value propositions
- **Quick actions**: Generate content from other intelligence tabs
