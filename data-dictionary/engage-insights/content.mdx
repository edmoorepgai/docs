## Overview

Content generation creates personalized sales materials based on the account's specific context, priorities, and your solution's value proposition. Each account includes 10 content credits.

## Generation Process

1. **Context Analysis**: Reviews account priorities and challenges
2. **Value Mapping**: Aligns your solution to their needs
3. **Tone Matching**: Adapts style to industry and persona
4. **Personalization**: Includes specific references and insights

## Quality Features

<Info>
All generated content includes:
- Strategic initiative references
- Industry-appropriate language
- Clear value propositions
- Compelling calls-to-action
</Info>

<Tip>
