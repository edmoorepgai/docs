## Best Practices for Content Usage

### **Customization Guidelines**

<Warning>
  Always review and customize generated content before use. AI-generated content should serve as a starting point, not a final product.
</Warning>

1. **Verify Information**: Confirm all account-specific details are current and accurate
2. **Match Your Voice**: Adjust tone and style to match your communication approach
3. **Add Personal Touch**: Include specific references to recent conversations or interactions
4. **Respect Preferences**: Consider the prospect's communication style and preferences

### **Content Quality Checklist**

Before sending any generated content:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Accuracy Check">
    Verify all company details, names, and specific information
  </Step>
  <Step title="Relevance Review">
    Ensure content addresses their specific situation and needs
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tone Alignment">
    Match the communication style appropriate for the relationship stage
  </Step>
  <Step title="Call-to-Action">
    Include clear, specific next steps that advance the sales process
  </Step>
  <Step title="Value Focus">
    Emphasize their outcomes and benefits, not your features
  </Step>
</Steps>
