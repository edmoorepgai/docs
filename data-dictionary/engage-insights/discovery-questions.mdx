## Overview

Discovery Questions are intelligently crafted questions designed to uncover pain points, validate assumptions, and advance sales conversations based on the prospect's specific context.

## Question Categories

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Strategic Alignment">
    Questions that connect to their strategic initiatives and goals
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Current State">
    Questions exploring their existing processes and challenges
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Impact Analysis">
    Questions quantifying the cost of current problems
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Decision Process">
    Questions understanding their evaluation and buying process
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Success Criteria">
    Questions defining what success looks like for them
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Example Questions

```markdown
Based on your digital transformation initiative mentioned in the annual report:
- "How is the current pace of your cloud migration affecting your ability to launch new services?"
- "What's preventing your team from achieving the 40% efficiency gain targeted for Q3?"
- "Which divisions are struggling most with the legacy system constraints?"
```
