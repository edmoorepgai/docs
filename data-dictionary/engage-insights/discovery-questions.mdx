Discovery Questions are strategically crafted, account-specific questions designed to uncover pain points, validate assumptions, explore business drivers, and advance sales opportunities based on each prospect's unique strategic priorities and business context.

Discovery Questions are generated from comprehensive account intelligence:

- Strategic priorities and business objectives
- Goals, challenges, and digital strategies
- Technology stack and organizational structure  
- Custom insights and recent developments

<Info>
  Discovery Questions are automatically generated based on the account's strategic priorities, goals, challenges, and digital strategies, ensuring relevance to their specific business context.
</Info>

## Where You'll See Discovery Questions

- **Engage tab**: Featured in meeting preparation and discovery planning
- **AI Assistant**: Request additional questions for specific scenarios
- **Content generation**: Incorporated into call scripts and meeting prep
- **Quick actions**: Access from account intelligence tabs
